Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products safe to burn for people with breathing conditions?

Yes, all of our candle products are safe to use for all types of people. However, please burn with caution.

How fast will I get my order?

Please allow 3 business days for order fulfillment and another 2-4 business days for delivery.

Is your packaging environmentally friendly?

All packaging is 100% recyclable, biodegradable, or, in some rare cases, post-consumer packaging reused from other vendors.

What if my candle was damaged in shipping?

If you receive a candle that has been damaged in shipping, please contact us within 24 hours of the delivery date with photos of the damaged items and the packaging materials. We can then work out a refund/exchange. After 24 hours of the delivery date, damaged goods will not be taken into consideration.

Do you offer returns?

Yes. If you receive your candle and are not 100% happy with them, please contact us within 72 hours of the delivery, and we will send you instructions on how to ship the items back to us safely. We will then issue you a full refund once we receive the item in 'like new' condition. The customer will be responsible for return shipping charges.

What are your candles made from?

The Candle Shop uses glass for the container, and our wax is soy-based and wax-free.

Why don't my candles smell?

We strive to infuse wonderful scents into all of our products. If you find that you cannot smell your candle, please send us a description of the scents and we will do our best to work out a refund
